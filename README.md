# PHP_Games_Website
This is an adventure game website with 2 PHP minigames, where the player can win (or lose) points for their accounts.

I used phpMyAdmin to manage the accounts and Photoshop and Illustrator to create all the graphics.

## Introduction

Au centre de la Terre... se trouve Noisuf, un royaume qu'on croit seulement exister dans les contes des enfants. Brûlant et mystérieux, Noisuf est la maison des créatures mythiques, nées de l'imagination la plus splendide des humains.

### English Version
In the heart of the Earth lies Noisuf, a kingdom that is believed to only exist in fairy tales. Blazing and mysterious, Noisuf is the home to mythical creatures, born from the most splendid human imagination.

Oh, dear traveler, you are probably here by chance. Consider yourself lucky... or unlucky. Truth be told, there is no difference, because many fall in love with the fortune of this kingdom.

If you have also chosen to explore this kingdom of fire for its fortune, the King of Noisuf, a generous man, offer you some presents. He also leads you to two roads, where you can find the fortune of your dreams.

## Game 1 - Chimera's Challenge
Choose a number between 2 to 12 (included) and throw a pair of dies. If the number you chose equals the value of the dies, you win. Otherwise, lose points.

## Game 2 - The Cave of Etrep
A dragon-themed virtual slot machine that costs points to play. The machine awards points to the player if all three slots (eggs) are of the same color.
